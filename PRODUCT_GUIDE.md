**Q: How much time should I spend per week?**
A: Start with your commitment from onboarding (typically 3-10 hours). Quality matters more than quantity.

**Q: Can I work on multiple projects?**
A: Yes! But focus on 1-2 at a time for best results. Complete before starting new ones.

**Q: Are my projects visible to colleges?**
A: Only if you choose to share your portfolio publicly. You control visibility.

**Q: What if I lose my streak?**
A: It happens! Start a new one. Your longest streak is saved for your record.

**Q: Can I delete a project?**
A: Yes, but consider archiving instead to preserve your work history.

**Q: Is the AI mentor safe?**
A: Yes! It provides educational support only and doesn't store personal data inappropriately.

**Q: How do I join someone's project?**
A: Find projects on the Discover page and send a collaboration request.

**Q: What happens when I level up?**
A: You get notifications, your profile updates, and you may unlock achievement badges!

**Q: Can parents/teachers see my work?**

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
